Features

No interface. Until you want one.

Flobro windows are pure content: no tabs, no address bar, no chrome. Move your mouse to the top edge and a slim toolbar slides in. Move away and it disappears.

Always on top

Your floating window stays above every app. Toggle it per window with the pin whenever you need normal stacking.

Distraction free

Frameless and silent. The hover toolbar gives you zoom, pin, minimize, settings and close, then gets out of the way.

Actually lightweight

Built with Tauri and Rust on the OS webview. A ~5 MB download that is easy on your memory and battery.

16:9 snap

One click snaps the window to perfect video proportions. Resize freely from any edge at any time.

Per-window zoom

Scale pages from 25% to 500% so a small floating window still shows exactly what you care about.

Open source

MIT licensed. Read the code, file issues, send pull requests.

How it works

Three steps to floating.

01

Paste a link

Open Flobro and drop in any URL: a video, stream, dashboard, docs page, anything on the web.

02

Hit Float

The page opens in a clean frameless window that stays on top of your other apps.

03

Get back to work

Move it, resize it, forget it. Hover the top edge whenever you need the controls.

Use cases

Not just for video.

Anything with a URL can float. These are the things Flobro users keep on screen the most.

Watch pipelines and dashboards

Keep your CI pipeline, Grafana board or status page in the corner of your screen. Green means keep working; red is impossible to miss.

Keep a chat in sight

Float WhatsApp Web, Slack or a live chat next to your work, without giving it a whole monitor.

Stream while you work

A match, a talk, a video: snap the window to 16:9, pin it on top and get on with your day.

Chrome extension

Float straight from Chrome.

The Flobro extension pops any tab, link or video out into a minimal window, right from the toolbar or the right-click menu. And with the desktop app installed, one click forwards the page to a true always-on-top window.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How do I keep a browser window always on top?

Open Flobro, paste a URL and hit Float. The window stays above every application on Mac and Windows until you unpin it. From Chrome, the Flobro extension floats the current tab and can forward it to the app with one click.

What is the difference between the Chrome extension and the desktop app?

The extension floats a tab in a minimal popup without any browser interface, but browsers cannot keep a window above other applications. The desktop app can, so the extension forwards pages to it for true always on top.

Is Flobro really free?

Yes. Flobro is free and open source under the MIT license, without accounts or ads. Development runs on voluntary donations.

Does Flobro track what I browse?

No. Anonymous usage statistics are limited to hostnames only, never full URLs, and can be switched off entirely. The details are in the privacy policy.
